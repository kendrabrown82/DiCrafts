This is a recipe for Tum'tumo

It's a commonly eaten dish from North East Africa, specifically Ethiopia and Eritrea

List of Ingredients

  • brown lentils
  • tomatoes
  • onions
  • jalepenos
  • garlic
  • salt
  • pepper
  • berbere a.k.a. the magic

In case you're wondering what berbere consists of, it's an assortment of spices including

  • coriander
  • korarima
  • ajwain
  • rue

Fortunuately the cooking process is fairly simple

  1. Place olive oil into the pot while on medium heat
  2. After a few minutes, placed the already chopped garlic and onions into the pot and occaisonally stir for 5 minutes
  3. Then place the rest of the ingrediates, in conjuction with water into the pot and lower the heat to low-medium
  4. Stir every 10 - 15 minutes for the next 3 hours
  5. Use salt and pepper to taste
